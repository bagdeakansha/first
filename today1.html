Cheescake Recipe

Hope u enjoy this Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • 3 Tbsp sugar
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 4pkg cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 4 eggs

Instructions

Heat over to 325F

Mix crumbs, sugar and butter. Press onto buttom of 8 inch pan.

Beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla with mixer until blended. Add eggs, 1 at a time,
mixing on low speed after till just blended. Pour over crust.

Bake for 55 minutes. Loosen cake from pan, cool before removing from pan.
Refrigerator 4 hours before serving.
